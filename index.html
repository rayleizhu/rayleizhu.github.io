ZHU LEI (祝磊)

I am a PhD candiate at City University of Hong Kong under the supervision of Prof. Rynson W.H. Lau. I also work closely with Dr. Wayne Zhang. I received my bachelor degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Dalian University of Technology (rank 1/102).

Currently, I focus on vision transformer design and large language model (LLM) inference. I am particularly intrested in hardware-aware deep learning (e.g. works like FlashAttention and TinyCudaNN). I believe in simple and scalable architectures and data-centric deep learning.

Previously, I have some experience in 3D vision (camera-LiDAR calibration and neural radiance field) and semi-supervised learning.

I am on the job market. If you have any openings related to large (vision-)language models or hardware-aware/efficient neural network design & deployment, I would like to get in contact!.
